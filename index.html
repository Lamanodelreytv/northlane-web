— How it works

A simple, predictable process.

From first message to delivered outcome, every engagement follows the same clear path. No surprises, no hidden steps — just a rhythm that lets us move fast while keeping you in the loop.

1
Day 0 · Free · 20 min

Discovery call

We hop on a short call so you can describe where you are and what's not working. We ask questions, take notes, and decide together whether there's a reasonable fit.

  • What you bring: a rough description of the problem and any context you can share
  • What we bring: honest feedback on whether we're the right partner for this
  • Outcome: a clear yes, no, or "talk to someone else" recommendation
2
Day 1–3 · Free

Scope & proposal

If we're a fit, we send a written proposal within 48 hours. It lists exactly what's included, what's excluded, what the timeline looks like, and what each party is responsible for. No ambiguity.

  • Deliverables with concrete acceptance criteria
  • Timeline with named milestones and dates
  • Fixed price or transparent billing structure
  • What you need to provide for us to start
3
Day 3–5 · Payment via Stripe

Agreement & kickoff

You sign the engagement letter, pay the first invoice through a secure Stripe link, and we schedule the kickoff. You receive a confirmation email with the project plan and a short list of information we need to get going.

  • Stripe-secured payment (cards, ACH, wire — your choice)
  • Signed engagement letter on record
  • Shared workspace created (Slack channel or email thread)
  • Kickoff call scheduled within 5 business days
4
Week 1 onward · Active work

Execution & async updates

We do the work, you stay informed. Weekly async written updates land in your inbox every Friday. Synchronous calls happen on agreed cadence. Questions and blockers get resolved inside 4 business hours.

  • Friday written status report (every week, without exception)
  • Live previews and access as things get built
  • Scope changes, if any, quoted before work continues
  • 4-hour response time on questions during business hours
5
Final milestone

Delivery & handover

At the end of the engagement, you receive everything we built — code, credentials, documents, access. We run a handover session so your team can own and extend the work independently. No vendor lock-in, ever.

  • Full transfer of code, accounts, and documentation
  • 30-minute handover walk-through with your team
  • 30-day post-launch support included
  • Optional ongoing support via a separate agreement

— About

Experts you hire by the service.

Northlane Digital is a small group of senior digital specialists who decided to work differently: clients book the specific service they need, pay for it, and leave with an answer. No retainers, no open contracts, no scope creep.

— Our story

We started Northlane because too many people were paying four-figure monthly retainers for advisory they needed twice a quarter. And too many other people were avoiding expert help altogether because every firm wanted a contract before a conversation.

The alternative we built is simple: publish each service with a fixed price, a fixed scope, and a written deliverable. You see exactly what you're buying before you buy. You pay once. You get the answer.

The team is deliberately small. Senior specialists handling each service themselves — no account managers, no juniors learning on your budget, no sales calls you didn't ask for.

— How we work

Every engagement is structured around three principles we refuse to compromise on.

  • Clarity first. If we can't explain it in plain language, we don't understand it well enough yet.
  • Ownership always. You own everything we build — code, content, accounts, access. No hostage situations.
  • Measurable outcomes. We agree on success metrics before we start and report against them every month.

— Refund & cancellation policy

Refund & cancellation policy.

Last updated April 2026. This policy applies to every purchase made through northlane.digital and is written to be read — not to hide anything behind legal boilerplate.

1. General principle

We want every client to feel that the value they received matches the price they paid. If something falls short, email hello@northlane.digital and we will work toward a fair outcome. Our default posture is to refund rather than argue.

2. One-time services (Payment Links)

This includes Discovery calls, Strategy sessions, Audits, Research reports and any other service bought directly through a Stripe Payment Link on the Payments page.

  • Before work starts: 100% refund, no questions asked. Requests processed within 3 business days.
  • Scheduled call not held: if we cancel and fail to reschedule within 14 days, 100% refund.
  • Client no-show: the session is considered delivered. A one-time rescheduling courtesy is offered if requested within 48 hours.
  • After delivery: no refund for completed deliverables. If the deliverable is defective, missing, or outside the agreed scope, we fix it at no additional cost.

3. Monthly plan (Continuous advisory)

  • 14-day satisfaction guarantee: within the first 14 days of a new subscription, you can cancel for any reason and receive a full refund.
  • After day 14: you can cancel at any time. The current billing cycle is not refunded, and no further cycles are billed.
  • No minimum term: the Continuous advisory plan has no minimum commitment — cancel anytime from your account.
  • Pause: you can pause a subscription for up to 30 days once per year, by written request.

4. How to request a refund

Email hello@northlane.digital from the address used to sign up, referencing the invoice number or the Stripe receipt. We confirm receipt within one business day and complete the refund within three to five business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method through Stripe.

5. Chargebacks

Please contact us before filing a chargeback with your card issuer. A direct conversation is almost always faster and fairer for both sides. Unjustified chargebacks filed without prior contact may be contested.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes are announced by email to active clients at least 14 days before taking effect. The version applicable to your purchase is the one published on the day of payment.

7. Questions

Email hello@northlane.digital. We read every message and respond within 4 business hours during our published office hours.
